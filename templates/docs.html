Overview

ChatForge is a comprehensive AI-powered chat platform that provides access to 100+ AI models from leading providers. Whether you're coding, writing, analyzing data, or just having a conversation, ChatForge brings the power of multiple AI models to your fingertips.

Getting Started

Important Requirement

You must have a Puter.com account to use ChatForge. The AI models are powered by Puter.com's API.

  1. Create Puter Account: Visit puter.com and sign up for a free account.
  2. Sign Up on ChatForge: Create your ChatForge account to get 100 daily credits and save your chat history.
  3. Choose a Model: Click the provider button to select from OpenAI, Claude, DeepSeek, Google, or Grok.
  4. Start Chatting: Type your message and press Enter or click the send button.
  5. Manage Chats: Access your chat history from the sidebar. Edit titles or delete old conversations.

Puter.com Setup Guide

1 Visit Puter.com

Go to https://puter.com in your web browser.

2 Create Account

Click "Sign Up" and create your free Puter account with your email address.

3 Verify Email

Check your email and verify your Puter account by clicking the verification link.

4 Login to Puter

Sign in to your Puter account. Keep this session active while using ChatForge.

5 Use ChatForge

Now you can use ChatForge! The app will automatically connect to Puter's AI services.

Note: You need to be logged into Puter.com in the same browser to use ChatForge's AI features.

Copy Responses: Hover over bot messages to reveal the copy button for easy sharing.

FAQ

How do credits work?

Free users receive 100 credits daily. Each message costs 1 credit. Credits automatically reset every 24 hours.

Do I need a Puter.com account?

Yes, Puter.com account is required as ChatForge uses Puter's AI API. Create a free account at puter.com.

Can I use ChatForge without signing up?

You can use guest mode with limited credits, but you need a Puter.com account for AI features. Sign up on ChatForge for 100 daily credits and chat history.

Which AI model should I use?

It depends on your task. GPT models are great for general conversation, Claude excels at analysis, DeepSeek is powerful for reasoning, and Gemini is fast for quick responses.

Is my data secure?

Yes. Your conversations are stored securely and are only accessible to you. We never share your data with third parties.
